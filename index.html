About Calligraphy Creations

My business began in December of 2016. My mother received a heat press and a vinyl machine for Christmas that year. Mom received these things in hope that her and I could make our own shirts, vinyl decals, etc… I had recently learned calligraphy, so we had an idea of putting my handwriting on a shirt. We figured out how to do that, and I showed my friends and family. One of my friends loved the idea and wanted me to make her a shirt with my handwriting on it. I then made her a shirt and posted a picture of it on Facebook and Instagram. To sum it up, people went crazy. People absolutely loved the idea and wanted shirts for themselves! I then kept getting custom orders for different names, quotes, etc… So, I continued posting on Facebook and Instagram pictures of the shirts I had made. Honestly, orders kept piling up from there. At the time, I only had vinyl shirts, but I wanted to explore with another shirt making process of some kind. In April of 2018, I began a process called Sublimation. Sublimation is ink dyed into the shirt and is much different than vinyl. It has to be done on special blend shirts that are a lighter color. Sublimation can be very detailed and you cannot feel it on the shirt, whereas vinyl cannot be very detailed and you can feel it on the shirt. This small business journey has been so fun for me, and I can’t wait to continue growing it. Thank you all for your business!
